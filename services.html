Frequently Asked Questions

A standard therapy session typically lasts 50 minutes. However, we offer flexible options to accommodate your needs, including extended sessions if required.

The frequency of therapy sessions depends on your individual needs and goals. Many clients start with weekly sessions and adjust as they progress. We'll work together to determine the best schedule for you.

During your first therapy session, we'll discuss your reasons for seeking therapy, your goals, and any concerns you may have. We'll also go over our confidentiality policy and answer any questions you may have. It's an opportunity for us to get to know each other and establish a foundation for our work together.

The duration of therapy varies depending on the individual and their specific needs. Some clients may only need a few sessions to address a specific issue, while others may benefit from longer-term therapy. We'll regularly assess your progress and discuss the duration of therapy with you.

If you're unsure about therapy, we encourage you to reach out and schedule a consultation. During the consultation, we can discuss your concerns, answer any questions you may have, and determine if therapy is the right fit for you. It's important to find a therapist who you feel comfortable with and who can meet your specific needs.
